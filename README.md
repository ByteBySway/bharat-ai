# 🌍 BharatAI — Apna AI, Apna Haq

> **Tagline:** AI for every Indian, in every village, for every dream.

BharatAI is a single, premium web super-app tailored specifically to address the unique challenges faced by rural communities across India. It aggregates **7 distinct modular portals** under one responsive shell, featuring localized database systems, translation layers, offline queue synchronization, accessibility modes, and an **Expert AI Diagnostic System**.

Live URL: **[BharatAI Web Portal on Vercel](https://bharat-ik8n9nlck-swaybytes-projects.vercel.app)**

---

## 🌟 Hackathon-Specific Power Features

### 🏆 Impact Dashboard & Live Map
* **Public counters**: Live tracker showing total farmers helped, students guided, and jobs found.
* **Interactive State Map**: Clickable state selectors (Haryana, Punjab, Rajasthan, etc.) displaying localized regional impact stats and SHG savings.
* **Success Stories**: Carousels showcasing stories of change from community citizens.

### 🎮 Comprehensive Gamification System
* **BharatPoints Ledger**: Earn points dynamically for checking soil quality, answering quiz questions, running calculations, or registering artwork.
* **District Leaderboard**: High-contrast scorecard comparing top villages and blocks (e.g., Tigrana Village vs. Bhiwani Block) to foster community pride.
* **Referral Unlock**: Share custom codes to invite friends.
* **Monthly Challenges**: Interactive check-ins to build learning habits.

## 📦 The 7 Portal Modules & 100+ Features

### 1. 🌱 KisanAI (Farmers & Husbandry)
* **AI Crop Doctor**: Crop disease recognition and pesticide formulation generator with organic alternate remedies.
* **Cost-Profit Calculator**: Estimate seasonal crop sales revenue vs fertilizer/seeds and labor costs.
* **Live Mandi Chart**: Graphical crop price trends over the last 6 months.
* **Cattle Disease AI Detector**: Describe animal symptoms to get veterinary diagnostic reports.
* **Milk Yield Feed Optimizer**: Fodder vs water milk production optimization inputs.
* **Goat & Poultry Blueprints**: Business plans for launching animal cooperatives.
* **Drone Spraying Guide**: Safety nozzle heights and gallons/acre calculators.
* **FPO & Cold Storage Radar**: Mock geolocation map showing nearest storage distance marks.
* **Carbon Credits Estimator**: Earn annual money through sustainable farming acres.

### 2. 🎓 SkillBridge (Students & Literacy)
* **IIT/ISRO Mentors**: Book sessions or consult curriculum specialists.
* **Career Roadmaps**: Detailed roadmaps for digital, office, and supervisor roles.
* **Study Quiz & Countdowns**: Multiple choice assessments to build learning streaks.
* **NCERT Digital Reader**: View standard chapter structures for classes 10 & 12.
* **Handwriting to Text Scanner**: Simulated Note scanner.
* **Parent-Teacher Dispatcher**: Weekly progress summary generator.
* **Mid-Day Meal Tracker**: Log meal details and calorie compliance indicators.
* **Dropout Risk Analyzer**: Risk matching percentages based on attendance numbers.
* **Smartphone Basics Tutorial**: Safe Google search tricks and browser lock verifications.

### 3. 🏥 HealthGuide (Health Care)
* **Symptom Severity Rating**: Classifies symptoms into Urgent, Standard Care, or Home Remediation.
* **Drug Interaction Checker**: Generic drug combinatorics checker to prevent conflicts (e.g., Aspirin + Warfarin).
* **Maternal Advisor**: Slide through pregnancy weeks (1-40) for vaccination timelines and nutritional checkups.
* **WHO Growth Standards**: Compare infant age, weight, and height against national curves.
* **Sleep Quality & Fatigue Index**: Calculate sleep efficiency percentages.
* **Ayushman Bharat Card**: Check local eligibility for cash-free health schemes.

### 4. 💼 RozgarAI (Jobs Board)
* **Rural Job Openings**: Find local MGNREGA projects or remote data entry supervisor jobs.
* **Freelancing & Gig Hub**: Walkthrough guides for Fiverr, Upwork, and digital skillsets.
* **AI Resume & Cover Letter Builder**: Create English/Hindi CVs in under 30 seconds.
* **Mock Interviews**: Interactive chat panels conducting job interviews.

### 5. 🏪 DukaanAI (Small Business & Finance)
* **Digital Ledger Book**: Track credits, income, and expenses with profit charts.
* **GST Compliant Invoicing**: Output receipts with 18% CGST/SGST and print layouts.
* **Shop Logo Designer**: Design and download customized shop logos as PNG files.
* **FD vs Savings Calculator**: Compare compound yields.
* **Jan Dhan Account Guide**: Eligibility guides for overdraft facilities.
* **Zero ITR tax assistant**: Planner for small earners to file ITR-1.

### 6. 👩 ShaktiAI (Women Empowerment)
* **SHG Registrar**: Manage self-help group rosters, savings balances, and DAY-NRLM schemes.
* **Folk Art documentation**: Upload and preserve region's artwork.
* **Handicraft Pricing Multiplier**: Recommended retail values calculator.
* **Dialect Dictionary**: Register regional words to preserve dialects.
* **Emergency SOS Console**: Pinpoints coordinates and dispatches SMS alerts.

### 7. 🌐 SamajAI (Community Notices & Transit)
* **Village Board**: Public announcements and classified listings.
* **Shared Carpooling Coordinator**: Shared rides planner.
* **Bad Road Reporter**: File road grievances with the Gram Panchayat.
* **PM Awas Yojana Eligibility**: Matching profile checks.
* **Rent Agreement Generator**: Interactive agreement templates.
* **WhatsApp Fake News Detector**: Pasted forwards analyzed for scam warning flags.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
* **UI/Styles**: Tailwind CSS CDN, Lucide Icons, Google Fonts
* **Charts**: Chart.js
* **Backend Integration**: Supabase (REST APIs) & Local Storage
* **AI Model Engine**: Rule-Based Expert AI Parser (local fallback) & Claude 3.5 Sonnet (API configurable)
